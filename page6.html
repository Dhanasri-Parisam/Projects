Video Editing Resources

  • Beginner to Advanced Video Editing YouTube Channels:
    • Peter McKinnon - Tutorials, vlogs, and filmmaking tips.
    • Film Riot - Action-packed tutorials and filmmaking challenges.
    • Cinecom.net - Focuses on cinematic techniques and storytelling.
    • Every Tuesday - Tutorials on motion graphics and animation.
    • Adobe in a Minute - Quick tutorials on Adobe Creative Cloud apps.
  • Free Assets (Footage, Music, Sound Effects):
    • Pexels Videos - Free stock footage.
    • Pixabay Videos - Free stock footage and music.
    • Videvo - Free stock footage and motion graphics.
    • FreeSound - Free sound effects and audio samples.
    • Bensound - Royalty-free music.
  • Specific Software/Tools YouTube Channels:
    • CapCut:
      • CapCut Official - Official tutorials and updates.
      • Search "CapCut tutorials" on YouTube for many user-created tutorials.
    • Premiere Pro:
      • Adobe Premiere Pro Tutorials - Official tutorials from Adobe.
      • Search "Premiere Pro tutorials" on YouTube for a wealth of resources.
    • Canva (Thumbnail Maker):
      • Canva Design School - Tutorials and design tips for Canva.
      • Search "Canva thumbnail tutorial" on YouTube.
  • Adobe Premiere Pro Assets:
    • Motion Array - Stock footage, templates, music, and more (paid, but often has free trials/assets).
    • Adobe Exchange - Plugins, extensions, and other assets for Adobe Creative Cloud apps.
